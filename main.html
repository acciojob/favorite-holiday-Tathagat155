Uttarakhand


Uttarakhand, known as the "Land of the Gods," is a beautiful blend of spiritual sites, natural landscapes, and adventure. Visitors can explore iconic religious sites like Kedarnath and Badrinath, enjoy the serene beauty of the Himalayas, and experience thrilling activities in Mussoorie and Nainital. Wildlife enthusiasts will love Jim Corbett National Park, while the Ganga and Yamuna rivers begin their journey here.

  1. Nainital for its scenic lake
  2. Mussoorie as a popular hill station
  3. Haridwar and Rishikesh for spiritual and adventure activities
  4. Jim Corbett National Park for wildlife encounters
  5. Badrinath (as part of the Chota Char Dham pilgrimage) for religious significance
