Uttrakhand


Uttarakhand is known as the "Land of the Gods" and offers a blend of spiritual sites, natural beauty, and adventure, making it an ideal destination for diverse travelers. Visitors can explore iconic religious sites like Kedarnath and Badrinath, experience the tranquility of the Himalayas through its snow-capped peaks and lush forests, and seek adventure in popular spots like Mussoorie and Nainital. The state also attracts wildlife enthusiasts to Jim Corbett National Park and serves as the origin for major rivers like the Ganga and Yamuna.

  1. Nainital for its scenic lake
  2. Mussoorie as a popular hill station
  3. Haridwar and Rishikesh for spiritual and adventure activities
  4. Jim Corbett National Park for wildlife encounters
  5. Badrinath (as part of the Chota Char Dham pilgrimage) for religious significance
